# Changelog

## v1.0.0
- Initial release
- Added 5 companion pet types:
  - Companion Drone (speed buff)
  - Companion Crawler (mining buff)
  - Companion Floater (inventory buff)
  - Guardian Bot (defense buff)
  - Scout Drone (resource detection)
- Pet following AI with orbit behavior
- Unique movement styles per pet type
- Configurable keybinds (Comma/Period)
- Research unlock: Companion Technology (150 Purple cores)
- Crafting recipes for all pets
